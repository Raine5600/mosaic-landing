Privacy Policy

Your thoughts
belong to you.

We built Mosaic on a simple belief: a note-taking app should never need to read your notes. Here's exactly how we protect your privacy.

Last updated: March 16, 2026 · Effective: March 16, 2026

The short version
  • All audio transcription and AI categorization happens entirely on your device. Your recordings never leave your phone.
  • We do not collect, store, or sell your voice recordings, transcriptions, or personal notes — ever.
  • The only personal data we may hold is your email address if you joined our waitlist.
  • iCloud sync is optional, entirely your choice, and governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • No third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, no trackers of any kind in the app.
  • You can request deletion of your waitlist email at any time by contacting us.
Section 01

Who We Are

Mosaic is a voice note application developed for iOS. When this policy refers to "Mosaic," "we," "us," or "our," it means the Mosaic development team. When it refers to "you" or "your," it means you as a user of the Mosaic app or visitor to our website.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the Mosaic app and the Mosaic waitlist at our website (mosaic-app.com). If you have questions, please contact us using the details at the end of this document.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect as little information as possible. Here is the complete list:

  • Waitlist email address. If you submit your email through our website to join the Mosaic waitlist, we store that email address so we can notify you at launch. This is the only personally identifiable information we hold about you.
  • No in-app personal data. The Mosaic app itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All app data — your recordings, transcriptions, categories, and notes — lives exclusively on your device.
We do not use any analytics tools, crash-reporting SDKs, advertising networks, or behavioral tracking systems inside the Mosaic app. If you download the app, we receive no signal that you did so, nor any data about how you use it.
Section 03

How Your Data Is Processed

Every core feature of Mosaic — audio recording, transcription, and AI categorization — runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device frameworks (including Apple's Speech Recognition framework and on-device machine learning models). At no point does your audio or text leave your device to reach our servers.

Specifically:

  • Audio recordings are captured locally by your device's microphone and processed in memory. The audio file is stored in your app's sandboxed local storage.
  • Transcriptions are generated on-device and stored in your app's local SwiftData database. They are never transmitted to any external server.
  • AI categorization is performed on-device using a local machine learning model. Your note content is never sent to a cloud AI service for classification.
  • iCloud Sync (if enabled by you) uses Apple's CloudKit infrastructure to sync your notes between your own Apple devices. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We never have access to it.

When you delete a note in Mosaic, it is immediately removed from your local database. If you have iCloud sync enabled, the deletion propagates to your other devices via Apple's infrastructure. We do not retain deleted data.

Section 04

Waitlist Email Addresses

If you submit your email on our website, we use that address only to:

  • Notify you when Mosaic is available on the App Store.
  • Send you occasional product updates prior to launch (no more than once per month).
  • Offer early access, beta invitations, or launch discounts.

We will never sell, rent, share, or transfer your email address to any third party. We will never use it to send you advertising for other products or services. We use a reputable email service provider to send these communications; your email address is shared with that provider solely for this purpose and subject to their data processing agreement.

You can unsubscribe from waitlist emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly at the address below. Upon unsubscription, your email will be deleted from our records within 30 days.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

The Mosaic app contains no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting tools.

Our website may use third-party hosting infrastructure (such as a CDN or static host). These services may log standard server access data (IP address, browser type, timestamp) as part of normal HTTP infrastructure operation. This data is not linked to you personally and is not used for profiling or advertising. It is subject to the hosting provider's own privacy policy.

Apple's App Store and Apple's iOS framework are not considered third parties under this policy. Your use of any Apple service is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

Mosaic is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with their email address, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

Section 07

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data under applicable law (including GDPR, CCPA, or similar legislation). Because the only personal data we hold about most users is a waitlist email address, exercising most of these rights is straightforward:

  • Access. You can request confirmation of whether we hold your email address.
  • Deletion. You can request that we delete your email address at any time. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Correction. You can ask us to correct an inaccurate email address.
  • Portability. You can request a copy of any data we hold about you.
  • Objection. You can opt out of our waitlist emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days.

All data generated by your use of the Mosaic app — recordings, transcriptions, categories └ is stored exclusively on your device and under your control. You can delete it at any time within the app or by deleting the app from your device.

Section 08

Data Security

App data is protected by iOS's standard sandboxing and data protection mechanisms. Notes stored on-device are encrypted using Apple's data protection APIs tied to your device passcode. We do not have access to this data and cannot decrypt it.

For waitlist emails, we use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS) and access controls to protect the data held by our email service provider.

No method of data storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting waitlist emails, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify waitlist members by email.

Your continued use of Mosaic after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Previous versions of this policy are available upon request.

Section 10

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please reach out to us. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.
