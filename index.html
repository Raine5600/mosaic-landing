How it works

Three seconds to captured,
zero effort to organized.

Mosaic removes every friction point between having a thought and having it organized. Just speak.

01
🎙
Tap & Speak
Hit the record button and speak naturally. No commands, no syntax. Just talk like you're leaving yourself a voicemail — in any language.
02
✨
AI Categorizes Instantly
Mosaic's on-device AI reads your transcription and assigns it to one of 8 smart categories — Idea, Task, Memory, Question, Note, Inspiration, Urgent, or Personal.
03
🎨
See Your Canvas
Each thought becomes a color-coded card on a beautiful masonry canvas. Your mind, visualized. Filter, search, or just scroll through your world of ideas.

Features

Built for how you actually think.

🧠
On-Device AI
All transcription and categorization happens on your device. Your thoughts never leave your phone — privacy by design, not just by policy.
🎨
Visual Canvas
A masonry grid of color-coded cards. Scroll through your entire thought world at a glance. Filter by category, search by content, or just browse.
⚡
Instant Capture
One tap to record. Three seconds to save. The fastest path from thought to organized note you've ever experienced.
🌐
Multi-Language
Think in any language. Mosaic transcribes and categorizes in 50+ languages, then displays your notes in your preferred language.
⌚
Apple Watch App
Capture from your wrist. Raise, speak, done. Your note syncs to the canvas in under 5 seconds — perfect for when your phone is in your pocket.
📊
Smart Insights
See patterns in your thinking. Which categories do you capture most? When are you most creative? Your mind, analyzed and visualized.
